Rangasthalam teaser: Ram Charan’s sound engineer avatar is quite intriguing

A bearded, rugged, lungi- wearing Chitti Babu (Ram Charan) is Rangasthalam’s engineer. With a temper always close to boil, Chitti Babu ‘sees’ sounds. And thus, in an ironic christening, he is called the sound engineer. Rangasthalam’s one-minute teaser shows a lot of promise and has much to excite the watcher. From Ram Charan’s nonchalantly aggressive demeanour to the rich colour shades, the teaser gives an intriguing peek into a place which calls a hearing-impaired engineer, ‘sound engineer’.

Bharat Ane Nenu first oath: Mahesh Babu film will be launched like never before

Superstar Mahesh Babu’s MB 24, directed by Siva Koratala, is coming out with their ‘First Oath’ this Republic Day at 7 am. While the ongoing trend is to announce the name of a film with a poster or a video teaser, the makers of MB 24, for the first time ever, are planning to unveil the title of the movie in an exciting way. An exclusive audio will be broadcast on all radio stations across the Telugu states, and on all major music platforms - Lahari Music, Apple Music, Saavn, Gaana, Wynk Music, Hungama, Google Play Music, Jio Music, Idea Music along with the official social media platforms of the movie and the production house. This kind of title announcement is the first of its kind in India, and will be a treat for not just superstar fans, but for all the movie lovers.

I'm ready to give my blood for Telangana': Pawan Kalyan

Popular actor and Jana Sena Party chief Pawan Kalyan on Tuesday said that he was ready to give his blood for Telangana, a state he had once protested against, during bifurcation.
Stating that Andhra Pradesh and Telangana were no different, the actor-politician said that in both the states his party would do responsible politics.
